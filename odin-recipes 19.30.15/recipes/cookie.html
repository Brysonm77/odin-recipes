Amazing Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookies can be eaten at any point in the day so why not learn how to make them

Sometimes cooking can be overbearing and scary. That's why this page is here to encourage and help some fellow food lovers learn how to bake and cook. What can be better than learning how to make the most amazing cookies. Not only can cookies be made for breakfast, desert, or even as a snack, but they can also be so diverse. Changing out certain ingredients can make a chocolate chip cookie into a brownie cookie, or a sugar cookie etc.

How can you make Cookies when you don't have any ingredients. Good thing for you I have everything you need

  • 1 cup of soften butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of chopped walnuts

Great now you have the ingredients but what do you do with all these items. Check below and you'll be making cookies better than a michellen star chef

  1. Preheat your oven to 130 degrees F(175 C)
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar
  3. Beat in one egg at a time then proceed to stir in the vanilla extract
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  5. Add butter along with salt
  6. Stir in flour, choclate chips, and nuts
  7. Degrease pan and proceed to pour spponfuls into the pan
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes or until the pan is nicely browned

Pro-tip: based on how chewy or hard you want the cookies either take out earlier or keep in longer.
